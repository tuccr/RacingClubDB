AI tools, specifically ChatGPT, were used in several supporting roles during the development of this project.

AI was used to generate the initial version of the mock data (mockData.sql) to populate the database for testing purposes. After generation, all data was reviewed, modified, and validated by the team to ensure it was accurate, relevant, and appropriate for the database design.

AI was also used to assist with debugging during development, particularly when resolving errors related to database connections, SQL execution, and backend integration.

Additionally, AI was used to help draft and refine some SQL queries. These queries were reviewed, tested, and adjusted by the team to ensure correctness and alignment with the project requirements.

All core aspects of the database system — including the ER diagram, schema design, normalization (up to Third Normal Form), table structures, relationships, constraints, and final implementation — were designed and implemented by the team.

AI was used strictly as a support tool to assist with development, debugging, and efficiency, and all final work submitted reflects the team’s understanding and validation.
